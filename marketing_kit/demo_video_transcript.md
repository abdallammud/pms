## Slide Design Guidelines

All slides should follow these design rules:

- **Background:** Dark navy gradient (`#1d3354` → `#162844`) or white with navy accents
- **Font:** Inter or Manrope (bold for headings, regular for body)
- **Accent colour:** `#2e62a8` for highlights, icons, and call-to-action text
- **Logo placement:** Top-right or centre on title/closing slides
- **Transitions:** Smooth fade or subtle slide — no flashy effects
- **Icons:** Use minimalist line icons (Bootstrap Icons style) in white or accent blue

## Freelancer Delivery Notes

### Screen Recording Instructions

1. **Browser:** Use Google Chrome in full-screen mode (F11) with the bookmarks bar hidden
2. **Resolution:** Set browser zoom to 100% on a 1920×1080 display
3. **Demo Data:** The system should be pre-loaded with realistic mock data (properties, tenants, leases, invoices, payments) — do NOT use empty screens
4. **Mouse Movements:** Move the mouse smoothly and deliberately. Pause on key elements for 1-2 seconds before clicking
5. **Speed:** Don't rush. Each screen recording segment should feel natural and unhurried
6. **Notifications:** Disable all OS notifications, close other apps, ensure no pop-ups appear during recording

### Slide Design Instructions

1. Slides should be created at **1920×1080** (same as screen recordings) for seamless transitions
2. Use **Canva**, **Figma**, or **Adobe After Effects** for slides
3. Match the Navy + White + Accent Blue palette provided in the product description document
4. Use the provided logo files (`kaad_pms_logo.png` for dark backgrounds inverted white, `kaad_pms_logo_white.png` for dark backgrounds)
5. Keep slides clean and minimal — no stock photos unless absolutely necessary
6. Use **fade** transitions between slides and screen recordings (0.3–0.5s)

### Audio Instructions

1. **Voiceover:** Professional male or female voice, confident and clear tone, moderate pace
2. **Background Music:** Low-volume, non-distracting corporate track (royalty-free)
3. **Suggested Music Sources:** Artlist, Epidemic Sound, or YouTube Audio Library
4. **Volume Balance:** Voice should be clearly dominant over background music at all times
